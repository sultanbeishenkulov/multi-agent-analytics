# Product Catalog

## Software Products
- **MetricsDash**: Business intelligence and dashboard platform. Top revenue generator. Targets Enterprise and SMB segments.
- **DataViz Pro**: Advanced data visualization tool. Strong in APAC and Nordics markets.
- **QueryBuilder**: SQL query builder with AI assistance. Popular with developer teams.
- **NoteStream**: Collaborative note-taking and documentation tool.

## Hardware Products
- **RackStore 8TB**: Enterprise NAS storage device. High unit price, lower volume.
- **VaultGuard**: Hardware security key and encryption device. Strong enterprise demand.

## Services
- **Architecture Review**: Professional services engagement. High revenue per order.
- **Enterprise SLA**: Premium support contract with guaranteed response times.
- **Admin Bootcamp**: Training program for system administrators.

## Pricing
- Software products: $200–$5,000 per license
- Hardware: $500–$8,000 per unit
- Services: $2,000–$20,000 per engagement

## Margins
Software products carry the highest margins (85–92%). Hardware margins are 20–40%. Services margins are 50–70%.
