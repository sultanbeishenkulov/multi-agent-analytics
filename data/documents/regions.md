# Regional Market Context

## Regions
- **North America**: Largest market by revenue. Strong Enterprise segment. Primary currency: USD.
- **EMEA** (Europe, Middle East, Africa): Second largest. Mix of Enterprise and SMB. Strong in financial services.
- **APAC** (Asia Pacific): Fastest growing. Strong Consumer and SMB. Key markets: Japan, Singapore, India.
- **ANZ** (Australia & New Zealand): Mature market, high LTV customers, strong Enterprise penetration.
- **Nordics**: Small but high-value. Highest AOV globally. Strong DataViz Pro adoption.
- **LATAM** (Latin America): Emerging market. Primarily SMB and Consumer. Price-sensitive.
- **Middle East**: Growing. Primarily Enterprise. Government and energy sector focus.
- **Southeast Asia**: High growth. Mixed segments. Strong mobile-first Consumer base.

## Revenue Targets by Region (Annual)
- North America: $500M
- EMEA: $350M
- APAC: $280M
- ANZ: $150M
- Nordics: $80M
- LATAM: $60M

## Regional Sales Notes
- Enterprise deals in ANZ and Nordics typically close in Q4.
- APAC Consumer growth driven by self-serve and low-touch sales.
- LATAM campaigns perform best on Social and Affiliate channels.
