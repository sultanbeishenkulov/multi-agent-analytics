# Key Performance Indicators

## Revenue KPIs
- **MoM Growth Target**: 5% month-over-month revenue growth across all segments.
- **AOV Target**: $4,000 per completed order company-wide.
- **Campaign ROI Threshold**: Any campaign with ROI < 0.5 is considered underperforming.

## Customer KPIs
- **LTV Target**: Enterprise $500K+, SMB $100K+, Consumer $10K+.
- **Churn Threshold**: Customers inactive for 6+ months flagged for re-engagement.
- **Ticket Resolution SLA**: Critical = 4h, High = 8h, Medium = 24h, Low = 72h.
- **CSAT Target**: 4.0+ average across all priorities.

## Campaign Performance Benchmarks
- Email channel: highest ROI historically, best for Enterprise re-engagement.
- Social channel: best for Consumer acquisition.
- Search channel: best for SMB inbound.
- Affiliate: best for LATAM and Southeast Asia markets.
- Display: lowest ROI, used for brand awareness only.

## Reporting Periods
- Monthly reporting: 1st of each month.
- Quarterly business review: end of March, June, September, December.
- Annual targets set in January for the full calendar year.
